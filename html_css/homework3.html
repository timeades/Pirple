Lemon Drizzle Cake

Lemon Drizzle Cake with its beautiful, light sponge and fresh, zesty flavours is a timeless bake that we can't seem to tire of.

This small-batch version means that you can enjoy it even more often!

The origin of lemon cake is unknown. It is probably derived from the pound cake, which was first baked in England around 1700.
A pound of each ingredient was used to make a simple, heavy cake to feed large groups of people.
